About bw CIRT

The Ministry of Transport and Communications (MTC) developed the National Cybersecurity Strategy. BOCRA is responsible for the Botswana Computer Incident Response Team (bw CIRT) which serves as the national point of contact for cybersecurity incidents.

Services

!

Incident Response

Coordinated response to cybersecurity incidents affecting Botswana's critical infrastructure and digital assets.

🔍

Vulnerability Assessment

Identification and analysis of security vulnerabilities in organizational systems and networks.

📢

Threat Intelligence

Collection and analysis of cybersecurity threats relevant to Botswana's digital ecosystem.

🎓

Awareness and Training

Cybersecurity awareness programs and training for government agencies and private sector.
