Equipment Certification for Botswana

Type Approval

Important Notice

BOCRA is mandated by Sec 84 of the CRA Act to Type Approve communications equipment that may be connected, used or operated to provide broadcasting or telecommunications services in Botswana.

What is Type Approval?

Type approval is the process of certifying that telecommunications and radio communications equipment complies with relevant technical standards and regulations before it can be imported, sold, or used in Botswana.

Why Type Approval?

  • Ensure equipment meets international standards
  • Protect consumer safety
  • Prevent interference with other radio services
  • Ensure network compatibility
  • Compliance with Botswana regulations

Application Process

Step 1: Document Submission

Submit technical documentation including test reports, specifications, and user manuals.

Step 2: Technical Evaluation

BOCRA reviews submitted documents and evaluates compliance with applicable standards.

Step 3: Laboratory Testing

Equipment may require testing at accredited laboratories to verify compliance.

Step 4: Certification

Upon successful evaluation, BOCRA issues type approval certificate.

Equipment Categories Requiring Approval

  • Mobile phones and tablets
  • Radio transmitters and receivers
  • Satellite communication equipment
  • Wi-Fi routers and access points
  • Bluetooth devices
  • Television receivers
  • Satellite TV dishes
  • Any equipment emitting radio frequencies
