Legislation

Legislation

Communications Regulatory Authority Act (CRA)

The Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority (BOCRA) is an independent communications regulatory authority established through the Communications Regulatory Authority Act 2012 (CRA) on 1 April 2013 with the mandate to regulate the communications sector in Botswana.

Electronic Records (Evidence) Act

BOCRA also has a mandate under the Electronic Records (Evidence) Act No 13 of 2014 that deals with the admissibility of electronic evidence in court, to establish an approved process for the production of electronic documents and also certify electronic records systems for purposes of integrity.

Electronic Communications And Transactions Act, 2014

The Electronic Communications and Transactions Act, 2014 mandates BOCRA to carry out accreditation of the secure digital signature service providers and administration of the take down notices. The Act facilitates e-commerce and gives electronic signatures the legal equivalence of hand written signatures.

Universal Access And Service Fund

BOCRA has entered into a memorandum of agreement with the Universal Access and Service Fund, where the UASF has BOCRA as the Manager of the Fund to provide investment and management services to the UASF.
