About .bw ccTLD

Section 38(1) of the Communications and Regulatory Act No.19 of 2012 (CRA) mandates Botswana Communications Regulatory Authority to administer the .bw country code top-level domain (ccTLD).

Domain Registration

The .bw domain is the official country code top-level domain for Botswana. It is managed and administered by BOCRA in accordance with international best practices and ICANN regulations.

Registration Services

  • Second-level domain registration (e.g., example.bw)
  • Third-level domain registration (e.g., example.co.bw)
  • Domain name transfer services
  • DNS hosting and management
  • Domain renewal and management
