Projects

BOCRA's ongoing and completed projects

BOCRA Projects

Ongoing

National Broadband Plan Implementation

Implementation of the national broadband plan to enhance connectivity across Botswana, focusing on underserved areas and rural communities.

  • Phase 1: Infrastructure deployment in rural areas
  • Phase 2: Urban connectivity enhancement
  • Phase 3: Last-mile connectivity
Ongoing

5G Spectrum Auction and Licensing

Preparation and execution of 5G spectrum allocation for enhanced mobile broadband services.

Ongoing

Universal Access and Service Fund (UASF)

Management and distribution of funds to support universal access to communications services in underserved areas.

Completed

Digital Migration

Successfully completed the transition from analog to digital broadcasting, ensuring improved quality and coverage of broadcast services.

Completed

Number Portability Implementation

Implementation of mobile number portability allowing consumers to keep their numbers when switching service providers.

Planned

IoT Regulatory Framework

Development of regulatory framework for Internet of Things (IoT) devices and services in Botswana.

Ongoing

Cybersecurity Capacity Building

Training and capacity building initiatives to strengthen national cybersecurity capabilities and protect critical infrastructure.
